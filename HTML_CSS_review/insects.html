Why Are Pollinators So Important?

Plant Pollination Cycle

Bees, beetles, butterflies, bats, hummingbirds, and many other species play a crucial role for the sustainability of the environment. Pollinators move pollen from the stamen, the male part of the flower, to the stigma, the female part of the same flower. Without them, this process cannot happen naturally!

Save The Pollinators!

Pollination is an essential ecological survival function; without it, all of earth's terrestrial ecosystems would not survive! So do your part and plant our wildflowers in your community today!
